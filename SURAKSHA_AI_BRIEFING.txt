  1. THE MISSION: PROTECTING THE PROTECTORS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Suraksha AI is a high-performance, medical-grade security platform designed 
specifically for the unique challenges of healthcare environments. 

Our mission is to safeguard "Healthcare Heroes" (Doctors and Nurses) while 
ensuring the highest standard of patient safety. Suraksha AI transforms existing 
hospital CCTV infrastructure into an active, intelligent shield that detects 
violence, negligence, and safety hazards in sub-second response times.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  2. HYBRID NEURAL ARCHITECTURE (NEW)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We have moved beyond simple cloud-only detection to a powerful DUAL-LAYER stack:

  LAYER 1: LOCAL PROXIMITY (YOLOv11)
  → High-speed object detection for people, fire, and suspicious motion.
  → Runs locally to ensure zero-latency monitoring.
  → Continuously feeds the Real-time Event Stream.

  LAYER 2: COGNITIVE REASONING (Groq Llama 3.2 Vision)
  → Intelligent scene analysis to understand complex human behavior.
  → Reasoning engine for "Why is this a threat?" explanations.
  → Multimodal logic for detecting aggression, negligence, and falls.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  3. UPDATED PLATFORM CAPABILITIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  3.1 UNIFIED MONITORING CENTER (Live + Archive)
      → A single "Command Center" that merges Live CCTV feeds with analyzed 
        Archive recordings.
      → One-click toggle between "Live Stream" and "Incident Playback".
      → Instant Alert Injection: Archive incidents trigger sidebar alarms in 
        real-time, perfectly synced with the video playhead.

  3.2 NEURAL FORENSIC ANALYZER (Consolidated)
      → The central hub for processing new clips or reviewing existing evidence.
      → Real-time "Neural Scan" visualization during video processing.
      → Automatic timestamp generation with AI-written incident descriptions.
      → Direct evidence library integration for legal and HR reporting.

  3.3 HOSPITAL-SPECIFIC THREAT DETECTION
      • AGGRESSION: Instant detection of physical violence in triage/ER zones.
      • FALLS: 24/7 detection of patient falls in corridors and wards.
      • FIRE: Real-time visual fire/smoke detection across all critical zones.
      • INFANT SECURITY: Monitoring maternity exits for unauthorized movement.
      • STAFF NEGLIENCE: Flagging duty abandonment or sleeping in high-risk units.
